Our Story

Mission & Vision

At Mendron LTD, our mission is to redefine facility management through innovative solutions that create safer, more efficient, and sustainable environments for our clients.

We envision becoming the most trusted name in facility services by consistently delivering excellence and building lasting partnerships.

Our History

Founded in 2025 by Andrew Afodoanyi and Abraham Doku, Mendron LTD began as a small maintenance provider with just three employees. Today, we serve several commercial clients across the Ghana with dedicated professionals.

Our growth has been fueled by our commitment to quality service and our ability to adapt to the evolving needs of modern facilities.
